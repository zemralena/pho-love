Our story

A father's dream,
served in a bowl

At Pho Love, everything we serve is crafted with love and care. Since 2025 we've been proud to share the vibrant flavors of Southeast Asia — authentic Vietnamese cuisine that warms the heart and soul.

Our story begins with a father's dream. A Vietnamese and Laotian immigrant whose life was forever shaped by the Vietnam War of the 1960s, he was displaced from home as a boy — home became something you carried with you, not somewhere you could stay. In the 1980s he came to the United States with almost nothing and built a life the honest way, one job at a time, for years laying hardwood floors with steady hands and long days. Through all of it, his love for food never wavered, and he held onto a quiet dream: to share the cherished dishes of his childhood with others.

In 2025, he stopped waiting. He turned to his son and said it plainly: "One day, I am going to do it." ♥

His son, fresh out of Penn State, came home to build it with him — a father with a recipe and a son who knew how to run a room. Two generations, one kitchen.

We believe great food starts with the best ingredients. That's why we use only the freshest, highest-quality produce, meats, and spices in everything we make. From our fragrant, ten-plus-hour broths to our hand-prepared dishes, freshness is at the core of every bite. Nothing rushed, nothing faked.

For us, food is more than just a meal — it's a way to bring people together, fostering connection, community, and celebration. When you dine with us, you're not just enjoying a meal; you're sharing in a tradition of love, culture, and culinary excellence.

The name says the rest. Everything here is made pho love, served at 220 Park Hills Plaza in Altoona. We invite you to experience the difference that freshness and heart make. Come hungry. Leave family.
