Our story

A father's dream, served in a bowl

At Pho Love, everything we serve is crafted with love and care. Since 2025 we've been proud to share the vibrant flavors of Southeast Asia — authentic Vietnamese cuisine that warms the heart and soul.

Our story begins with a father's dream. A Vietnamese and Laotian immigrant whose life was forever shaped by the Vietnam War of the 1960s, he was displaced from his home — but his love for food never wavered. He dreamed of sharing the cherished dishes of his childhood with others.

For us, food is more than a meal. It's how we bring people together — connection, community, and celebration. When you dine with us, you're sharing in a tradition of love, culture, and culinary excellence.
